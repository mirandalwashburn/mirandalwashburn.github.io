Online Banking & The Desert Financial Mobile App

Now that you have the basics down, you’ll want to set up contactless banking using Online Banking and the Desert Financial Mobile app. These convenient tools allow you to access your accounts 24/7, move your money around and apply for new loans on any Wi-Fi-enabled device. Go ahead, you got this!

How to Sign Up for Online Banking

  1. Visit DesertFinancial.com. On the right, click Sign Up Now.
  2. Choose Register as an Individual.
  3. Read and accept the disclosure and scroll down to check the box marked I Agree. Click Continue.
  4. Provide your information to confirm your identity. Click Continue.
    (Your account number is listed on your new account paperwork. Only the first 10 numbers of your account/ member number are required — omit the hyphen and four zeroes.)
  5. Choose a username and select if you would like to receive your temporary password by email or text. Click Continue.
  6. Check your email/text messages. Enter your temporary password to authenticate and click Log In.
  7. Create a New Password and Confirm. Click Continue.
  8. Next, confirm your contact information and click Continue.

That’s it! Log in to Online Banking anytime, anywhere at DesertFinancial.com or use our handy mobile app to manage your accounts. Now, about that app …

Downloading the Desert Financial Mobile App

Visit your device’s app store and type Desert Financialin the search box.

  1. Choose Desert Financial Mobile and Install.
  2. Click Open and answer the location prompt.
  3. If you already have an Online Banking login, enter your username/password here and click Log In. If not, follow the easy instructions above.
  4. Select Snapshot if you’d like to view your balances immediately whenever you open the app.

That’s it! You’re ready to start banking on the go.

Using Online Banking and the Mobile App

Inside the app, click the menu bar in the top left corner to see all the things you can do. We call these widgets, but you can call them tasks, menu items or your “can-do” list — whatever makes your soul sing.

In Online Banking, these widgets appear in grey boxes on the left side of the page (or in the drop-down menu on mobile).

Here are some things you can do inside the app AND in Online Banking. Feel free to look around!

  • View Balances: Click Dashboard to see an overview of your account balances and transactions. If you choose Snapshot, you’ll also get a sneak peek at your account balances whenever the app opens, a pretty sweet feature that most bank apps don’t have!
  • View Accounts: To view specific account details, click Accounts in the main menu, then choose your account.
  • See a Transaction: Click on a transaction for specifics, like where and when that purchase was made. ($118 at Olive Garden??? Guess it’s macaroni-from-a-box this week!)
  • Find Your Account Number: Click on the Details tab. Scroll down to your Incoming Wires Account Number. Use the first 10 digits – basically, everything before the dash.
  • Transfer Money: Click Transfers in the Main Menu. Choose the accounts you want money deducted FROM and sent TO. Enter the transfer amount, review and click Complete Transfer. Easy-peasy.
